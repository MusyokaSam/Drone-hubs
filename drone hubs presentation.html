The Drone Hub
A Dedicated Facility. Five Functions.
✈
Commercial Operations
Precision agriculture, infrastructure monitoring, topographic surveying, and data collection. Multiple revenue streams generating commercial activity from day one.
🎓
Training & Certification
Accredited UAS operator courses delivered by programme graduates. A domestic pipeline of certified local operators expanding beyond the initial cohort each year.
🔧
Maintenance & Airworthiness
Inspection, repair, and fleet management for own and third-party operators. In markets where certified maintenance is absent, this is essential infrastructure.
🔬
Research & Development
Applied R&D in collaboration with regional universities and agricultural institutions. Drone solutions adapted for local terrain, crops, climate, and operating conditions.
⚙️
Local Manufacturing
Locally assembled drone systems developed alongside commercial platforms. Reducing import dependency and building the foundation for a domestic UAS industry.
🌐
Angani-Managed Network
Every Hub operates within a shared network of standards, curricula, procurement, and R&D outputs. Graduates return to a defined role in an operational facility.

About Angani
Independent Aviation Development
Angani Aviation Engineering
anganiavi.com
Angani Aviation Engineering is an independent aviation development company focused on building local aviation capacity in emerging markets through programmes, partnerships, and infrastructure that transfer expertise, technology, and operational capability.
The Drone Hubs Initiative is one of Angani's flagship initiatives, built on the conviction that the growth of aviation technology in Africa must be driven by Africans, on African soil, sustained by locally trained and locally certified professionals.
